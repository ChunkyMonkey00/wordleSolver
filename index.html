Wordle List Filter



  • Input yellow letters into the "Include Letters" section.
  • Put gray letters in the "Exclude Letters" section.
  • Put green letters and their position (0-4, instead of 1-5) in the "Filter by Position" section.
  • Put yellow letters and their position (0-4) in the "Filter Not at Position" section.
  • Both "Filter by Position" sections auto-clear. Enter 1 letter at a time.
  • Repeating a letter has no effect, so do not worry about that.
  • Reset the list if you mess up or if you are done.
